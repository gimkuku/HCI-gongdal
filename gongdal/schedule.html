Manage your TIME, Win the CONTEST

Hello. We are team no.11. Christina, one of the today’s job seekers, prepares various contests to get a job. But for each content, she has difficulty managing due date because of different schedules and preparations. So, she is finding some a website that manages the schedule and organize portfolio. Finally, she found GONGDAL that manages the time of the contest and her portfolio after the contests! Our team, 디자인은 누가하죠, is going to develop the GONGDAL that integrates of contests information, gives reviews of the specific contest to win, manages the documents and organizes schedule of the contest to fit Christina’s or other’s need. After managing time, users can win the contest!

Here are the sketches we made by brainstorming. We picked those two because it is easy to manage time schedule with contest information. It is convenient to use and see the schedule visually because it is based on the calendar design. Users can share the time management schedule with teammates for scheduling. For the personal page, there are a clander shared by teammates. It shows the duration date of the contests. If you click the month, you can see the schedule of contest that you want to join. In this page, you can manage your schedule by week and the contests. Next, if you click see priority button, you can see the comparing board. In the comparing board page, people can set and see the expected time and get the priority.

Next, if user clicks contest, users can see the contests information including Popular contests, recent contests and some information including preferences and view times. User can search the contests too. And if user click schedule page, user can manage their contests including number of contests, need time, priority and the schedule calendar related with the contests. If you go to the contest pages and click one of the contests, then people can see more detail by clicking the banner box. On this page, people can the contest at a glance.

By the way, these two designs have pros and cons for each one. Firstly, the main page is good for watch contest list, shows start date and deadline of the contest on the calendar, and classifies contests by popularity or type. However, it is difficult to manage time schedule and cannot put another personal schedule. Secondly for calendar, users can easily manage schedule with expectation time and priority and share and edit the contest date and schedule with teammates. It is hard to see the contest information by type, though. In other words, it cannot classify by the type or popularity.

firstly, for the simple task, user checks the schedule of the contest. On the main page, user can check the contest information including start and end date. Then if user click ‘schedule’, then she can see monthly and weekly schedule of the contest. Then, if user clicks specific month, then she can check weekly schedule calendar. If user click “contest” on the banner box, then they can check the priority in comparing board. Next, Participating in the contest is moderate task. On the main page, user can search for contest. User can check the contest info and chooses one that she wants to join. Then, user can put schedule of the contest on personal page. And then users can check all the contest lists that that she joins. Click the contest button on the banner and see the priority of contest. And then after participating in they contest and can manage the portfolio. Lastly, for the complex text, user can manage time and portfolio after participation. Click the “Schedule” button on the banner bar to manage time, schedule. On this page, user can edit contest list and each schedule. On the schedule table, there are the timeline and shows tasks with the text box. Click specific month to check weekly schedule with calendar. User can check number of contests and needed time. And click either of icons to see the sorted schedule. click “contest” button to see contest list and sorted by priority. Finally, user can manage portfolio and share information after the contest.
